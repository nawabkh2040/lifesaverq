Create A QR Code

Why Create an Emergency QR Code?

An emergency QR code, when placed in your vehicle or carried with you, can be scanned by first responders or Good Samaritans in case of an accident. It contains vital information such as your name, emergency contact details, blood group, and medical history.

How to Create Your QR Code

  1. Click on the "Create A QR Code" button above.
  2. Fill in your personal details and medical information.
  3. Click "Generate QR Code" to create your unique QR code.
  4. Download and print the QR code or save it on your phone.
  5. Place the QR code in your vehicle or keep it with you for emergencies.
